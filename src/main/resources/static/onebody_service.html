Physiotherapy

At One Body Physiotherapy we provide the highest quality of care that is tailored to you. We want to get to know you as a person as we understand that everyone is unique with different lifestyles which may impact on your pain. Therefore, every session at One Body Physiotherapy you can expect 1-to-1, full 30 minutes with your experienced therapist where you can share your story and receive treatments from the following:

Education

Understand your pain, so you are in control

Exercise

Precise exercises to fix and prevent the pain from returning

Manual Therapy

Hands-on treatment to manage your symptoms

Dry Needling

Use of acupuncture needles to relax your muscles

Medicare

Chronic Disease Management (CDM) Program or previously known as Enhanced Primary Care (EPC) is a government funded program where if you have a chronic medical condition (minimum 6 months), you may be eligible for Medicare rebates for your physiotherapy consultations, up to 5 sessions. Speak to your General Practitioner (GP) to see if you are eligible and obtain a referral to One Body Physiotherapy.

Who is eligible

Patients with chronic medical condition that has been present for 6 months

Referral

Obtain a CDM referral to One Body Physiotherapy from the GP then contact the clinic for an appointment

Payment

We bulk bill the Medicare rebate but there is a small out of pocket payment of $25 per visit

Workers Compensation

If you have sustained an injury from your work or at your workplace, you may be eligible for physiotherapy services under WorkCover WA. One Body Physiotherapy will provide an effective treatment to swiftly fix your pain and for you to return to work confidently.

Who is eligible

Worker who has sustained a workplace injury

Referral

GP referral letter/
Claim details (insurer name, claims number, case manager)

Payment

There is no out of pocket payment/
We will directly charge the insurer

Motor Vehicle Accidents

Patients who have sustained an injury from motor vehicle accident are eligible to receive physiotherapy services to help them return to pre-injury function. Compulsory Third Party (CTP) insurance will cover the cost of the services if you are eligible. Please refer to Insurance Commission of Western Australia (ICWA) to start the claim process.

Who is eligible

Patient who has sustained an injury from motor vehicle accident

Referral

GP referral letter/
ICWA claim details (claims number, case manager)

Fees

There is no out of pocket payment/
We will directly charge the insurer

Booking

Department of Veteran Affairs

Department of Veteran Affairs (DVA) card holders are eligible for physiotherapy services to manage their pain and to enhance their quality of life. General Practitioners (GP) can provide a referral for physiotherapy services up to 12 sessions each referral.

Who is eligible

DVA card (gold/white) holder

Referral

GP referral for DVA physiotherapy treatment

Payment

There is no out of pocket payment
We will directly charge the DVA
