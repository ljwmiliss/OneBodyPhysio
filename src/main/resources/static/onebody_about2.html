Our Mission

  • At One Body Physiotherapy, we love to help people and we believe that we have been blessed to be able to give back to the people who are less fortunate around the world. We visit these people providing physiotherapy care and financial support on a yearly basis.
  • You can participate with us on this mission as you receive treatment at One Body Physiotherapy, we will give a portion of your fee to someone around the world who are in need. Read the stories (link) of the people you have helped.
